Our Certifications Explained

Click on a standard to learn more about our commitment.

HACCP (Hazard Analysis Critical Control Point)

The Hazard Analysis Critical Control Point is used as a utile to specify, evaluate and control any possible dangers occurring during the production process. This system is widely approved by many countries in its efficiency and safety control of the products for the consumers.

ISO 9001:2015

ISO 9001:2015 is the quality system used in quality assurance and process management in the organization of the Supplier or the manufacturer which can use as a guideline or condition in an international agreement between a buyer or a seller and a manufacturer or a supplier in order to make continuous improvement and satisfy the clients.

AAFCO (The Association of American Feed Control Officials)

Our production process is approved by the AAFCO. Our products have enough nutrition according to the National Research Center (USA). We have an effective selection of quality ingredients and use also a modern production process in order to produce qualitative pet food with adequate nutrition.

GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice)

GMP is the minimum obligations that have to be conducted in order that the food will be safe for human consumption. It is the readiness management of the production process status.

HALAL SUCI

Our products were passing through the mixing, seasoning, assembling and packaging processes in accordance with Islamic rules approved by Islamic Food Research Centre (Hong Kong) or IFRC Hong Kong which guarantees that our products can find acts with correctness, cleanness and safety under the Islamic religion.
